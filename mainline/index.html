Common questions

Things parents ask before they book.

How do I know whether my child actually needs tutoring?
That's exactly what the first call is for. We'll talk through current scores, goals, and timeline before you decide whether tutoring makes sense. If we don't think tutoring is the right move, we'll say so.
How does virtual tutoring actually work?
Sessions run over Zoom with a shared digital whiteboard and screen-sharing. Tutors mark up problems in real time and pull up Desmos, College Board materials, or whatever the student needs. Many families find it works just as well as in-person, without the driving.
Do I have to commit to a package up front?
No. We bill hourly as you go. Many families start with a few sessions to see how the fit feels, then continue if it's working. We'll never push a large package on the first call.
Should my student take the SAT or the ACT?
It depends on the student. Some score noticeably better on one than the other, and the best way to find out is a short diagnostic of each. We'll talk through this on the call and help you figure out whether the choice is settled or still open.
What does it cost?
We bill hourly as you go. Rates depend on tutor experience and the type of support your student needs, and we'll explain the exact rate before you decide whether to start. There's no large upfront package.
